do not train third-party models on it. Full export available any time
from _Settings → Data → Export_; deletion is permanent and immediate.

No spam, no abuse of the rate-limited surfaces, no use to defraud the
Czech tax administration. The latter is a both-of-our-problems clause.

Limited to fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim. Standard
SaaS-contract carve-outs (gross negligence, willful misconduct, breach
of confidentiality) apply.

Czech Republic. Disputes are heard by Czech courts.

## Changes to these terms

We notify workspace owners by email 30 days before any material change.
Continued use after the effective date is acceptance.
