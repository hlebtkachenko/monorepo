## Plans

| Plan       | Users         | Invoices / mo | Price                  |
| ---------- | ------------- | ------------- | ---------------------- |
| Free       | 2             | 50            | 0 Kč                   |
| Standard   | up to 10      | unlimited     | 290 Kč / user / month  |
| Pro        | up to 50      | unlimited     | 490 Kč / user / month  |
| Enterprise | custom        | unlimited     | contact sales          |

Storage, API, MCP, and webhooks are included on every paid plan. The free
tier caps API calls at 1 000 / month; paid tiers carry generous quotas.

## What counts as an active user?

A user who signed in at least once during the billing month. Accountants
invited as cross-workspace read-only viewers count once, even when they
access multiple client workspaces.

## Payment methods

Card (Stripe) for monthly invoices. Yearly invoices are issued for bank
transfer (CZK / EUR) by default; card is available on request. VAT is
added per the customer's tax status (B2B EU: reverse-charge; B2B CZ: 21 %;
B2C: per country).

## Invoices

Every monthly bill ships as PDF + ISDOC 6.0.1 to the billing email on the
1st of the month. Due in 14 days. Late payment triggers a soft suspension
(read-only access) after 14 days, hard suspension at 30, deletion at 60.

## Switching plans

Up- and downgrades take effect on the next billing cycle. Pro-rata refunds
are issued automatically for downgrades.

## Cancellation

_Settings → Billing → Cancel plan_. Workspace stays active until the end
of the paid period; data export remains available for 30 days afterwards.

## Refund policy

Pro-rata refund within 14 days of purchase. After that, no refund on past
periods.
