## 1. Create your workspace

Sign up at [app.afframe.com](https://app.afframe.com) with your work email. The
first sign-up under a domain becomes the workspace owner; teammates join via
invite from _Settings → Members_.

## 2. Add your organization

Open _Settings → Organization_. Required fields:

- **Legal name** (must match the entry in the obchodní rejstřík).
- **IČO** (8-digit business identifier).
- **DIČ** (VAT number; prefix `CZ` for Czech payers).
- **Fiscal year start** (default January; switch if you use hospodářský rok).

Afframe validates IČO + DIČ against the ARES public registry; mismatches block
saving.

## 3. Connect your bank

_Settings → Banking_ supports a per-account upload (CSV / GPC / ABO) and a live
connection through CSAS / Fio / KB / Raiffeisen open banking APIs. Live
connections refresh every 4 hours; uploads are idempotent on transaction ID.

## 4. Send your first invoice

_Invoices → New_. Fill in the counterparty (Afframe auto-completes from ARES),
items, VAT rate. Save as _draft_ to keep editing or _issue_ to lock and send.
Issued invoices are immutable; corrections go through a credit note.

## 5. Invite your accountant

_Settings → Members → Invite_. Pick the **accountant** role; they get the
read-only views into your books plus the export bundle. They can flip back to
their own workspace from the top-left switcher.
