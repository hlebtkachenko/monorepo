## What does Afframe cost?

A free tier (one workspace, two users, up to 50 invoices / month) plus paid
tiers from 290 Kč / user / month. Full pricing: [pricing page](https://afframe.com/pricing).

## Is my data my data?

Yes. Every workspace can export a full ZIP of its database (JSON + ISDOC +
PDF invoices) from _Settings → Data → Export_. The export is generated within
~5 minutes for typical workspaces. Deletion is permanent and immediate.

## Where is data stored?

EU. AWS Frankfurt (eu-central-1). The dev / staging environments are isolated
in the same region; nothing leaves the EU.

## Does Afframe replace my accountant?

No. Afframe is bookkeeping software; your accountant signs the tax return.
Afframe provides the export bundle your accountant feeds into their software
(Pohoda / Money S3 / Helios / Premier).

## Can I cancel any time?

Yes. The active workspace stays accessible for 30 days after cancellation;
during that window you can export your data, after which the workspace is
deleted.

## Is there a trial?

The free tier is the trial. No credit card required.

## Do you support multi-currency?

Yes. Source-currency entries are stored as-is; period revaluation uses the
ČNB daily rate. See [FX conversion](/accounting/fx).

## What about GDPR?

Afframe is the data processor; the workspace owner is the controller. The
DPA template is on the security page (TBD).

## Who do I contact about a security issue?

`security@afframe.com`. PGP key on the security page.

## Why don't I see my counterparty in ARES?

ARES indexes the obchodní rejstřík + the živnostenský rejstřík. Sole-trader
(OSVČ) records sometimes propagate slowly. Add the counterparty manually —
Afframe will reconcile on the next ARES sync.
