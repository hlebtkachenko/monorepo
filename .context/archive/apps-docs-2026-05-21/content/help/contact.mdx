## General support

`support@afframe.com` — product questions, bug reports, onboarding help.
Include your workspace slug (visible in the URL: `app.afframe.com/<slug>/...`)
and, where relevant, the `requestId` from any API error envelope.

## Billing

`billing@afframe.com` — invoices, plan changes, refunds, VAT documents.

## Security

`security@afframe.com` — vulnerability reports, security incidents.

PGP fingerprint and disclosure policy at the [security page](https://afframe.com/security).

## Status page

Live availability at [status.afframe.com](https://status.afframe.com).
Subscribe to the RSS feed or email-alerts list for incident notifications.

## SLA

Free tier: best-effort.
Standard / Pro: next business day.
Enterprise: per-contract SLA.

Support hours: business days, 09:00–18:00 CET. Critical security issues
get an out-of-hours response.
